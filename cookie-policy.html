1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies help a website to:

  • Function correctly and provide essential features
  • Improve security and prevent fraud
  • Analyse website usage and performance
  • Store user preferences and settings
  • Remember information about your visit
⸻

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies as they are essential for the website to work.

  • Session handling – to maintain your session while using the website
  • Security – to protect against fraud and ensure website security
  • Contact form functionality – to enable form submissions and prevent spam

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve the website's performance and user experience.

  • Website analytics – to understand how visitors use our website
  • Performance monitoring – to identify and fix technical issues
  • User experience improvement – to enhance website functionality

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

5. Cookie Control & Management

You have control over cookies. You can:

  • Disable cookies via browser settings – Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences
  • Delete cookies at any time – You can delete cookies that have already been set on your device
  • Block specific cookies – You can choose to block cookies from specific websites
  • Set cookie preferences – Many browsers allow you to set preferences for different types of cookies

Please note: Disabling or deleting cookies may affect website functionality. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

How to Manage Cookies in Popular Browsers

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies and site permissions
⸻

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies can be either:

  • Session cookies – Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies – Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

We use both types of cookies as necessary for website functionality and user experience.
